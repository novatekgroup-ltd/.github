# Contributing to NovaTek Group Ltd

Thank you for your interest in contributing to NovaTek Group Ltd. As a private consortium operating in highly regulated and security‑sensitive domains, we manage contributions through a controlled internal process.

## Internal Contributors (Team Members)

1. **Branching Strategy** – Use feature branches off `develop`. Branch naming: `feature/XYZ-123-short-desc`.
2. **Commit Messages** – Follow [Conventional Commits](https://www.conventionalcommits.org/).
3. **Pull Requests** – All PRs require review by at least one core maintainer and must pass automated security scans.

## External Contributors & Open Source

At this time, the majority of NovaTek's codebases are proprietary and not open for public contribution. However, we do welcome:

- **Security Vulnerability Reports** – Please follow the process outlined in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
- **Documentation Corrections** – If you spot a typo or inaccuracy in our public documentation, feel free to open an issue.

## Code Style & Standards

- **Markdown** – Adheres to `.markdownlint.json` configuration.
- **Docker** – Compose files follow the official specification.
- **Security** – All contributions must pass static analysis and dependency scanning.

## Questions?

For internal inquiries, contact the platform engineering team. For external security matters, email [security@novatekgroup.com](mailto:security@novatekgroup.com).
